Earth

Planets Planets are large bodies of rock, metal and gas that travel round star. Eight planets including Earth,orbit our Sun. The earth is the only planet at right position from Sun to sustain life.

Earth The Earth is huge, rocky ball spinning around in space. It is around 4.6 billion years old. Its surface is covered with large areas of land surrounded by sea. The layers of air around the Earth make up its atmosphere. In the atmosphere are the gases that all living things need to stay alive.

Moving Earth: About 220 million years ago all the continents were joined as one super continent called Pangea, this continent began to break up.

On the surface: Lots of different features cover the Earth's surface,such as snowy mountains, deep valleys, rocky deserts and enormous ice sheets.

Hot rocks and metals: The outer layer of the Earth is called the crust. It is between 30 and 50km thick below the land but only 5 to 10 km below the ocean and seas . The very centre of the Earth lies about 6400km below the surface.

Deep Down : Far below the surface of the oceans lies another landscape on the floor of the ocean. Here there are mountains, wide plains and slopes and deep sea trenches.

Natural disaster: An earthquake is a natural disaster. It can cause serious damages in just few seconds. It is the violent shaking of the rocks inside the Earth.

Earth Features There are many wonderful sights on the surface of our amazing planet. They include thundering waterfalls, steep cliffs by the seashore and huge caves deep underground. Some of these features took millions of years to form.
